About

Cezanne Camacho is a Stanford graduate, having received her Master’s in Electrical Engineering in June, 2015. A learner and an educator, she has done extensive biotechnology research in projects that range from synthetic biology to image processing. You can read about her past research, here. She has also taught programming courses in Python and in Java to students interested in building their own games and applications. She is currently teaching machine and deep learning topics at Udacity. Inspired by anyone with the drive and interest to learn, she aims to create more inclusive and effective STEM education.
